▮ FEATURES

WHAT'S IN THE BOX.

Built by people who reload saves a lot. No fluff, no charge per person, no "powered by AI." Just a save vault that works.

▮ VERSION HISTORY

EVERY UPLOAD IS A VERSION.

Scroll through every backup with file count, size, and what changed since last time. Hit Restore and it puts the save back AND marks it as current — no confusion.

▮ CO-OP LOCKS

ONE PERSON HOLDS THE WORLD.

Games like Factorio, Satisfactory, Valheim, and V Rising only have one live world file at a time. Whoever holds the lock can upload it; everyone else just downloads. Nobody using it? Grab the lock yourself — no more "who has the latest save?"

▮ ONLY WHAT CHANGED

TINY UPLOADS.

We only upload the files that changed, and skip the ones that didn't — even renamed files cost nothing extra. Easy on your internet, easy on your storage.

▮ PER-GAME RULES

PICK WHAT GETS BACKED UP.

Choose which files to back up and which to ignore. Skip the crash logs and screenshots, keep the save. Already set up for the 40+ games Checkpoint64 knows about.

▮ BACKGROUND APP

RUNS IN THE BACKGROUND.

A small, light app that barely uses any memory. Checks every 2 minutes and rests when nothing's changed. Won't get in the way while you're playing.

▮ LOGBOOK

WHO DID WHAT, WHEN.

Every upload, restore, and lock-grab gets written down in your group's logbook. Handy when your co-op partner blames you for the bad run.

▮ LOGBOOK · LIVE shared with your group

▮ FAQ

FREQUENTLY CHECKED.

WHAT IS A 'SAVE'?
Whatever your game writes to your hard drive. Checkpoint64 treats the files in a folder as one save and backs them up together. You point it at the folder and pick which files count. We’ve already set this up for 40+ popular games.
DO YOU UPLOAD WHILE THE GAME IS RUNNING?
Yes, carefully. Checkpoint64 never locks your save files. If the game is in the middle of saving when we check, we wait a moment and try again — no broken files, no crashes. Most games finish saving in a split second anyway.
WHAT HAPPENS IF MY CO-OP PARTNER OVERWRITES MY UPLOAD?
They can’t, on purpose. Only the person holding the lock can upload. If they want to push their version, they have to grab the lock first — and that warns you before it happens. Your last version stays safe in the history; you can always roll back to it.
DO I STILL NEED A DEDICATED SERVER?
For most groups, no. The whole point of a dedicated server is keeping your world online when the host’s PC is off. Checkpoint64 covers about 90% of that for a one-time fee: whoever wants to play grabs the lock, plays their session, then pushes the save back. A typical co-op group saves €120–€240 a year compared to renting a 24/7 server that sits idle 18 hours a day.
DOES THIS WORK FOR CONSOLE SAVES?
Only if you can get your console saves onto a PC — like emulators, Xbox or PS+ cloud save exports, and Steam Cloud through your PC. Checkpoint64 itself only runs on Windows, Mac, and Linux.
WHAT WILL IT COST?
Three tiers: Free (20 MiB, personal only), Lifetime (one-time payment, 5 GiB per space + 2 teams), and Pro (monthly, 50 GiB per space + 5 teams, for bigger crews). No charge per person on any tier. Final numbers aren’t set — sign up to the launch list and we’ll tell you before everyone else, plus lock you in at the early-bird price.
WHEN DOES IT SHIP?
Soon — we’re testing in private now and aiming for a public launch later this year. Get on the list and we’ll send one email the day the Windows / Mac / Linux versions are ready.
